Police Brutality and Racial Profiling

This website is to inform you of the problems and negative results that comes with police brutality and racial profiling.

Racial Profiling

What are the negative effects of this?

  • Discrimination
  • Stereotyping
  • Inequality of Race

Police Brutality

What are the negative effects of this?

  • Abuse of Power
  • Untrustworthy Law Enforcers
  • Your human rights violated

How can you help stop or decrease the amount of these actions from happening?

  • Use Social Media to help spread the information
  • If your community was affected by an officer of the law or racism, protest for them to be gone from your community
  • Email your local government for them to address the abuse of power
